Settings

Optional AI enhancement, your data, and research feedback. ArguMentor works fully without any of this.

Optional AI feedback (bring your own key)

By default ArguMentor uses a transparent, offline rule-based engine. If you add your own large-language-model API key below, the Workspace gains an AI review button and the Tutor answers through your model. If a request fails, ArguMentor silently falls back to the rule-based engine.

Privacy. Your key and settings are stored only in this browser (localStorage). Requests go directly from your browser to the provider — ArguMentor has no server and never receives your key or your writing.

Your data

All drafts, scores, teacher notes and survey answers are stored only in this browser. Export a copy for safekeeping or to hand to a teacher/researcher, or clear everything.

Tell us how ArguMentor is working for you

Four quick questions on usefulness, usability, learning support and ethical acceptability. Optional and anonymous; answers stay in your browser and are included if you export your data.
